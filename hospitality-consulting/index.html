PACKAGE 01 — SIGNATURE BEVERAGE PROGRAM

INFO /

This package is designed for hospitality concepts seeking more than a drinks menu — it delivers a fully realized beverage identity. QILOMBO works closely with ownership and leadership teams to design a beverage program that aligns with brand vision, guest experience, and revenue strategy.

From concept development to execution standards, every element is intentionally crafted to ensure the beverage offering is cohesive, scalable, and commercially effective.

DELIVERABLES /

Beverage Concept & Brand Alignment - Beverage Program Narrative: A defined creative direction that connects the beverage offering to the venue’s identity, cuisine, and target audience. - Flavor Architecture & Style Framework: Development of flavor profiles, category balance, and stylistic direction to guide current and future menu evolution. - Signature Drink Philosophy: Guidelines for hero cocktails, non-alcoholic offerings, and seasonal rotations.
Operational & Costing Strategy - Cost Analysis & Margin Targeting: Costing of each beverage with pricing recommendations aligned to profitability goals. - Ingredient Rationalization: A Strategic selection of spirits, modifiers, and house ingredients to minimize waste and streamline operations. - Supplier & Category Guidance: Recommendations for spirit categories and product positioning without brand exclusivity or sponsorship bias.
Training & Service Standards - Staff Training Session (On-Site or Remote): Structured training covering menu knowledge, preparation standards, and service storytelling. - Service Flow & Execution Guidelines: Best practices for speed, consistency, and guest engagement behind the bar. - Program Handover Documentation Guidance: A complete reference package enabling internal teams to maintain and evolve the program independently.

TIMELINE / 4–6 weeks, depending on concept complexity and collaboration cadence.

INVESTMENT / Starting at $7,999 USD

(Final pricing may vary based on scope, menu size, training depth, and on-site requirements.)

PACKAGE 02 — BEVERAGE REFINEMENT & DOCUMENTATION

INFO /

This package is designed for established hospitality venues with an existing beverage program that requires clarity, consistency, and professional documentation rather than a full redesign.

QILOMBO works to formalize and elevate your current beverage offering, ensuring recipes, presentation, and internal materials reflect a polished, intentional program that can be executed consistently across teams and shifts.

DELIVERABLES /

Beverage Documentation - Signature Recipe Compendium: Professional documentation of existing cocktails and beverages, including standardized measurements, preparation methods, glassware, and garnish specifications. - Execution & Consistency Guidelines: Clear standards to ensure uniform quality, speed, and presentation across service periods and staff members.
Brand & Presentation Essentials - Branded Beverage Presentation Deck: A clean, professional slide template for internal training, brand presentations, or partner communications. - Digital Content Templates: Editable social media templates designed to visually align beverage offerings with brand identity and enhance online presence.
Strategic Recommendations - Program Optimization Notes: Targeted insights on menu flow, balance, and efficiency, identifying opportunities for refinement without restructuring the entire program. - Visual & Language Alignment: Light adjustments to menu language and presentation to improve guest clarity and perceived value.

TIMELINE / 2–4 weeks, depending on menu size and documentation requirements.

INVESTMENT / $3,999 USD

(Ideal for single-venue concepts seeking professionalization without a full program rebuild.)
